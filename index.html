FreshStart Reentry Initiative

Your pathway to new opportunities after incarceration

Our Mission

FreshStart, powered by Zion Mission of Hope, is dedicated to helping formerly incarcerated individuals find meaningful employment and rebuild their lives with dignity and hope. We focus on connecting you to felon-friendly employers in the Charlotte area and beyond.

Job Categories

  • Warehouse
    Sample employers: Amazon, UPS, FedEx, Local Distribution Centers
  • Hospitality
    Hotels, Restaurants, Event Staff, Cleaning Services
  • Construction
    Local Contractors, Skilled Labor, Equipment Operators
  • Retail
    Stores like Walmart, Target, Dollar General
  • Food Service
    Fast Food, Catering, Cafeterias
  • Transportation
    Delivery Drivers, Trucking Companies (CDL opportunities)
